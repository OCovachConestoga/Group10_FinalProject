How to Play Spikeball:

Spikeball is a fast-paced 2-on-2 game played with a small round net placed between both teams.

Objective

Teams take turns hitting the ball onto the net so the opposing team cannot return it. The rally continues until the ball hits the ground, the rim, or a team fails to return it.

Basic Rules

  • Each team is allowed up to three touches per turn to return the ball.
  • The ball must be hit, not caught or thrown.
  • Once the serve is made, there are no boundariesâ€”players can move freely around the net.
  • A player may not hit the ball twice in a row.

Scoring

  • Games are typically played to 21 points and must be won by 2 points.
  • A point is scored when the opposing team fails to legally return the ball.

Tips

  • Use all three touches strategically to set up a strong return.
  • Communicate with your teammate.
  • Stay quick on your feet and be ready for 360-degree play.
